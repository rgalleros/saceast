About Us

Skeptron Alumni of Cebu – East (SAC East) is a distinguished brotherhood of long‑standing Alpha Kappa Rho (AKP) members, united by a shared commitment to unity, service, and leadership. Founded on the timeless principle Vincit Omnia Veritas — “Truth Conquers All” — our alumni chapter continues to uphold the values that have guided AKP since 1973. We exist not only to strengthen the bonds among alumni but also to extend our fraternity’s spirit into the wider community. Through outreach programs, medical missions, leadership seminars, and service projects, SAC East strives to embody the ideals of brotherhood, excellence, and compassion. Our mission is clear: to preserve the legacy of Alpha Kappa Rho while building a future where fraternity values inspire positive change in society. Together, we stand as a beacon of Brotherhood, Leadership, Service, and Excellence in Cebu and beyond.
